# Chapter 2: Overview of Humanoid Robotics

## Learning Objectives
- Understand humanoid robot structure
- Learn applications of humanoid robots

## Introduction
Humanoid robots are designed to resemble the human body in structure and movement. They aim to perform tasks similar to humans in real-world environments.

## Core Concepts
- Degrees of freedom
- Human-like joints
- Balance and locomotion

## Use Case
Humanoid service robots in healthcare, assisting nurses or carrying equipment.

## Summary
Humanoid robots mimic human form and behavior, enabling them to operate in environments designed for humans.
