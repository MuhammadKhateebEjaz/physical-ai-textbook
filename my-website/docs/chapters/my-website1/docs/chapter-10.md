# Chapter 10: Future of Physical AI & Humanoid Robots

## Learning Objectives
- Explore future trends in Physical AI
- Understand emerging technologies in humanoid robotics

## Introduction
Physical AI is rapidly evolving with new research, applications, and technologies. Future humanoid robots will be more intelligent, adaptive, and safe for human interaction.

## Core Concepts
- Ethical AI: Ensuring safety and responsible behavior
- Human-robot collaboration: Robots assisting humans in everyday and industrial tasks
- Advanced perception and learning capabilities

## Example
Robots collaborating with humans in healthcare, education, and manufacturing, learning from human actions and improving efficiency.

## Summary
The future of Physical AI and humanoid robots includes smarter, safer, and more interactive systems that enhance human life and productivity.
