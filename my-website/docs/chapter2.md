# Chapter 2: Humanoid Robotics Basics

## What is a Humanoid Robot?
A humanoid robot is designed to resemble the human body structure, enabling it to operate in human-centered environments.

## Key Components
- Mechanical structure
- Sensors (camera, IMU, force sensors)
- Actuators (motors, servos)
- Control systems

## Challenges
- Balance and stability
- Motion planning
- Energy efficiency
- Human-robot interaction
